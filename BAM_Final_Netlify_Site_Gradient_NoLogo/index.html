Who We Are

At Blake Austin Management (BAM), we don’t just manage talent — we build brands. We represent creators, athletes, and innovators shaping culture, offering a talent-first approach backed by strategic insights and creative execution.

Our mission is simple: empower our clients to build lasting personal brands that thrive far beyond a single campaign or platform. From securing world-class partnerships to crafting breakthrough content strategies, we help our talent create authentic, sustainable influence.

With deep relationships across media, entertainment, and lifestyle brands, BAM bridges the gap between visionary talent and global opportunities. We handle the strategy so our clients can focus on what they do best — creating, inspiring, and leading.

Our Pillars

  • Talent First — Every decision starts with your long-term growth.
  • Strategy Always — Data-driven insights meet bold creative vision.
  • Culture Driven — We partner with talent and brands shaping what’s next.

How It Started

Blake Austin Management was born from a simple vision: to create a talent-first agency built on personal relationships, strategic growth, and cultural relevance.

Founded by Blake Solnit, BAM began with a handful of creators and athletes who wanted more than representation — they wanted a partner. A partner who could champion their voice, amplify their impact, and help them build lasting personal brands that go beyond social platforms.

What started as a boutique approach has evolved into a full-service talent management agency, connecting our roster with global brands, creative opportunities, and next-level collaborations. While BAM has grown, one thing hasn’t changed: our commitment to authentic partnerships and helping talent set trends, not follow them.
